Guess the Number

About game

We have selected a random number between 1 and 100. See if you can guess it in 10 turns or fewer. We'll tell you if your guess was too high or too low.

Instructions:

  • The computer has picked a secret number between 1 and 100.
  • Your guess into the box and click Submit.
  • After each guess, you’ll be told if the number is too high, too low, or correct.
  • Keep guessing until you find the secret number!
  • You'll get 10 turns each guess
